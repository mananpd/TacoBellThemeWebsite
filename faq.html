Your Burning Questions Answered!

Got questions swirling in your mind like the melted cheese on a sizzling Mexican Pizza? We've compiled answers to the most commonly pondered queries about your upcoming Taco Bell adventure below.

Is this an actual Taco Bell restaurant?

Yes! We don't have the full corporate menu, but we've mastered a selection of iconic items just for you.

Can I customize my order to perfection?

You bet! Unleash your inner food architect. If we've got it in stock, your modifications are welcome.

I have dietary restrictions/allergies. Can you accommodate?

Kindly inform us of any dietary concerns upon arrival. While we'll strive to accommodate, please understand that cross-contamination is a delicious possibility in our humble cantina. For particularly challenging dietary requirements, we make an exception and permit outside food."

What kinds of games will we be playing?

Prepare for a delightful mix of party classics! Expect strategic wordplay with Codenames and other group games designed to entertain (and sometimes slightly embarrass) while your food is being prepared. Participation is encouraged, but not mandatory if you're deep in a food-coma prep.

Will I really hear the Taco Bell Chihuahua?

Yes. Gidget's spirit is strong here. Whether it's a ghostly "Yo Quiero Taco Bell!" or a phantom growl, don't be alarmed. It's just her way of saying she approves of your life choices (especially the ones involving cheese).
